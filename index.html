Chuletas Fritas

(Fried Pork Chops)

These pork chops (chuletas) are not only packed with flavor, but they are sooo juicy!

Ingredients

For Marinade

Instructions

  1. Combine all marinade ingredients in a large bowl and mix well
  2. Place pork chops into bowl with marinade and massage marinade into them so that they are fully covered
  3. Cover bowl and let marinate overnight in the refrigerator. If you can't marinate overnight - marinate for at least an hour
  4. When ready to begin cooking remove from refrigerator and let sit out for about 20 minutes
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  6. Heat frying pan on medium heat and once heated add olive oil
  7. Remove pork chops from marinade and dry them completely
  8. Place pork chops in frying pan and sear for about 2-3 minutes per side. Do not move them while they're searing
  9. Place pork chops onto baking sheet and put into oven to complete cooking (about 7 minutes)
  10. Remove from oven and cover them for about 5 minutes
  11. Serve and enjoy!
